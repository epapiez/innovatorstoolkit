SCHOOL LIFE

My academics at Michigan!

I am currently a senior at Michigan, and I am in the School of Information. I have minors in Entrepreneurship, STS (Science, Technology, and Society), and Polish. When I entered college, I did not really know what I wanted to do. I started off on track to be an Architecture major, but quickly realized it was not for me. At the same time, I took an introductory coding class and realized that technology would allow me to be both creative and analytical. I struggled deciding between Computer Science, Cognitive Science, and Information, but finally settled on the School of Information after realizing the class sizes were smaller and I could take both data analytics classes as well as user experience design classes.

EXTRACURRICULARS

What I'm passionate about and do in my free time

I am most heavily involved in the Panhellenic Association, where I previously served as the Vice President of Internal Relations. I have also held a variety of other positions within my sorority chapter, including Panhellenic Delegate, Graphic Design Chair, Bid Day Committee, Senior Experience Chair, and more. The reason for this is that I am very passionate of women's empowerment and confidence. As a female in STEM, supporting and empowering other women in extremely important. Freshman year, I was also in the Women in Science and Engineering Residence Program. There, I met my best friends and learned how important it is to have diversity in STEM fields. I also learned that it is important to be confident in sharing your ideas and being proud of your accomplishments. In my free time, I also love to do yoga, and am currently participating in a 30 day yoga challenge!

FAMILY

My family is extremely important to me!

My dad, my mom, and my sister are my best friends. Family is very important to me, and I try my best to stay in touch with them in college. My dad is named Radek, my mom is named Marlena, and my sister is named Adela. My parents both immigrated from Poland, so we are very close to Polish culture, which is why I am also minoring in Polish. My family loves to go hiking and participate in other outdoor activities together.
